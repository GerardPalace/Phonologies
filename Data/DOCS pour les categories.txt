Signification des categories assignes aux langues par rapport au nombre des
voyelles/consonnes

Consonnes (http://wals.info/chapter/1)

small (from 6 to 14 consonants)
moderately small (15-18)
average (22 ± 3)
moderately large (26-33), and
large (34 or more)


Voyelles (http://wals.info/chapter/2)

“average” 5 or 6 members
 “small” 4 or fewer
 “large” 7 or more
